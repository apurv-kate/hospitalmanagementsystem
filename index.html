Cosmetic & Plastic Surgery

Cosmetic & Plastic Surgery focuses on enhancing appearance and restoring function. It includes aesthetic procedures like facelifts, liposuction, and rhinoplasty, as well as reconstructive surgeries for burns, injuries, or birth defects. Our expert surgeons ensure safe care with natural-looking results.

×

Bariatric & Metabolic Surgery

Bariatric & Metabolic Surgery focuses on weight loss and metabolic health. It includes procedures like gastric bypass, sleeve gastrectomy, and adjustable gastric banding. Our experienced team provides comprehensive care for patients seeking sustainable weight loss solutions.

×

Fetal Medicine

Fetal Medicine specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of fetal conditions. It includes advanced imaging techniques, prenatal interventions, and collaboration with pediatric specialists. Our goal is to ensure the best possible outcomes for both mother and baby.

×

Breast Surgery

Breast Surgery encompasses a range of procedures aimed at addressing various breast conditions. It includes breast augmentation, reduction, and reconstruction, as well as treatments for breast cancer. Our skilled surgeons prioritize patient safety and aesthetic outcomes.

×

Cancer Services

Cancer Services provide comprehensive care for patients with cancer. It includes early detection, advanced treatment options, and supportive care throughout the cancer journey. Our multidisciplinary team is dedicated to improving patient outcomes and quality of life.

×

Gastroesophageal Disorder

Gastroesophageal Disorder focuses on conditions affecting the esophagus and stomach. It includes diagnosis and treatment of GERD, hiatal hernias, and other related disorders. Our specialists use advanced techniques to provide effective care and improve patient comfort.

×

Cardiac Sciences & CCU

Cardiac Sciences & CCU focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of heart conditions.

Gastroenterology

Gastroenterology specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of digestive system disorders. It includes conditions like acid reflux, ulcers, and inflammatory bowel disease. Our team provides comprehensive care using advanced diagnostic and therapeutic techniques.
